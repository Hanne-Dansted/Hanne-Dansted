healthy bunny signs

  • active and sociable
  • healthy fur
  • clear bright eyes
  • eats and drnks regularly
  • communicates by making soft noises
  • hops normally
  • normal stools

unhealthy bunny signs

  • prolonged diarrhoea/dirty bottom
  • overgrown teeth
  • heat stroke - panting, loss of consciousness or seizures
  • skin leisions
